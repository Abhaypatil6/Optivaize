# Pairing Wireless Buds Pro

1. Open the charging case with buds inside.
2. Hold the case button for 3 seconds until the LED blinks blue.
3. On your Windows laptop, open **Settings → Bluetooth → Add device**.
4. Select **Optivaze Buds Pro** and confirm pairing.

If pairing fails, forget the device in Bluetooth settings and repeat from step 2.

Firmware updates require the Optivaze mobile app (iOS/Android).
