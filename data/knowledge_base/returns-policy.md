# Returns & Refunds Policy

You may return most products within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund if unused and in original packaging.

Opened items may receive a **restocking fee of 15%** unless defective.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after we receive the return.

Defective items: full refund or replacement at no restocking fee when verified by support.
